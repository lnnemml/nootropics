# Brand Hierarchy — NORA vs. NeuroDrive

> Confirmed by Anton, 2026-06-29. This corrects an assumption baked into
> earlier wiki pages (notably `home-page-copy-v1.md`, now superseded) —
> re-read this before writing copy for any page.

## The structure

**NORA — Nootropics Research Alliance** is the platform/company brand.
It owns:
- The website itself (home, mission, education/community content)
- The shared visual identity — the "N" mark, the full design system
  (see [`design-system.md`](./design-system.md)) extracted from Claude
  Design's first pass
- The multi-product future (NeuroDrive is product #1; more will follow
  under the NORA umbrella)

**NeuroDrive** is the first *product* under NORA — not the brand itself.
On the physical label, this hierarchy is already visible: the bottle
leads with the NEURODRIVE™ wordmark and product-specific subtitle, while
NORA's "N" mark sits at the top of the label and "Nootropic Research
Alliance" appears as a small endorsement line at the bottom — the
manufacturer/research-alliance credibility mark, not the headline brand.

## What this means for copy, page by page

| Page | Voice | Subject |
|---|---|---|
| Home / mission (`(marketing)`) | **NORA** | The alliance's mission, evidence-over-hype philosophy, education. NeuroDrive is mentioned as the flagship release, never the main subject. |
| `(shop)/neurodrive` advertorial | **NeuroDrive**, inside NORA's visual system | Full product-specific belief chain, bromantane mechanism, objections, proof — everything in `beliefs-and-objections.md` / `messaging-angles.md` belongs *here*, not on the home page. |
| `(shop)/products/[slug]` generic template | Product-specific | Same pattern as the advertorial, more concise — every future product follows this template under the same NORA visual system. |
| Global nav / footer | **NORA** | Nav wordmark reads "NORA," not "NeuroDrive," everywhere except inside the NeuroDrive product pages themselves (where a sub-brand lockup is appropriate, mirroring the label). |
